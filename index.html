Our place

A warm welcome in Golden Bay

Good as Gold Cafe is a relaxed, friendly spot in Tākaka — the kind of place where the coffee's good, the food's honest, and you're never rushed. Whether you're a local grabbing your morning flat white or a traveller passing through the Bay, there's a table and a warm welcome waiting.

We keep things simple and do them well: good coffee, food made fresh, and service with a smile. It's what's earned us a loyal following and a solid run of great reviews.
