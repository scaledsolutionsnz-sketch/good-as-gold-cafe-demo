Privacy Policy

How Good as Gold Cafe handles the information you share with us through this website.

Last updated: 2026. This is placeholder content provided as part of a website demo and should be reviewed by the business before going live.

1. Who we are

Good as Gold Cafe ("we", "us", "our") is a cafe in Tākaka, Golden Bay. This policy explains how we handle personal information collected through this website, in line with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

2. Information we collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us — for example, when you submit feedback through our form, we collect your name, email address, rating and message. We do not require you to create an account to browse this site.

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to your feedback or enquiry
  • To improve our food, coffee and service
  • To contact you if you've asked us to

We will never sell your personal information.

4. How feedback is sent

Our feedback form uses a third-party form service (FormSubmit) to deliver your message to us by email. Your information is transmitted for that purpose only.

5. Third-party services

This website may link to Google Maps and Google Reviews. When you follow those links, the relevant provider's own privacy policy applies.

6. Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we handle your details with care.

7. Your rights

You may ask us to access or correct the personal information we hold about you. Contact us using the details below.
